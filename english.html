About me

Thank you for visiting my website!

I am Mariana Martinez, from Venezuela but living in Belgium since 2006. I started a massage practice in 2017 and obtained several diplomas in my field from 2015 in Syntra Pelt, Genk and Hasselt. My passion is to meet people, listen to their stories and help them. For me, a massage is a way to offer help and care to others to release them from their stress, emotional blockages and muscle pain. MMzen means the one who is specialized in giving beauty and strength to your body and soul with the different techniques of Mariana Martinez to get a total zen. I enjoy giving massages and I am also happy when my clients are relaxed again. I work in a professional way to help you find your inner balance. So... choose a massage that's right for you and that will help you feel better. You are always welcome in my cozy practice and enjoy a new experience to recharge your body's battery.

Hartelijke groeten, warm greetings, saludos calurosos!
